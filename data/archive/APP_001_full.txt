[Project_ID] APP_001
[Project_Name] Web3 羽球社 (Web3 Badminton Club)
[Applicant] 許明恩 (hi@blocktrend.today)
[Source_Link] https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1XMtMJdOXo6vaXZiiZ0hfqqZRdh-BUSCGXq8dmFqC-BI/edit#gid=872891188

[Core_Concept]
結合運動與 Web3 技術的社群實驗，呼應 Ethereum Localism。透過羽球降低 Web3 入門門檻，將區塊鏈工具（錢包、支付、POAP）融入日常生活。

[Technical_Stack]
- Framework: Next.js, React
- Web3: WagmiConnect, Web3Modal, MetaMask, WalletConnect
- Tools: TailwindCSS, Luma API (Event management), Signal (Communication)
- On-chain: Ethereum (POAP), USDT/USDC/ETH (Payments)

[Execution_Details]
- Weekly Events: 2-hour sessions, doubles matches.
- Entry Barrier: Must use Signal group; only crypto payments accepted (250 NTD equivalent).
- Rewards: POAPs for attendance; accumulated POAPs redeem limited team uniforms.
- Governance: Future DAO integration for club decisions.

[Budget_Plan] Total 30,000 NTD
- Tournament prizes/venue: 3,000
- Professional coaching: 4,000 (2 sessions)
- Team uniforms: 10,000 (30 pcs)
- Refreshments: 3,000
- Manager allowance: 10,000

[Expected_Outputs]
- 12+ event records/photos
- POAP series design
- Open-source "Web3 Sports Club" operation manual
- Active Signal community
