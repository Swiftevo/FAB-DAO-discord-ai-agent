[Project_ID] APP_003
[Project_Name] 大量交織+地球公物 (MEGAWEAVING + Earth Goods)
[Applicant] 蕭有志 (Yu-Chih Hsiao)
[Source_Link] https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1XMtMJdOXo6vaXZiiZ0hfqqZRdh-BUSCGXq8dmFqC-BI/edit#gid=872891188

[Core_Concept]
將擁有 9,800+ 成員的 Facebook 資源共享社團轉化為具備 Web3 存證功能的資料庫。將共享物件（相機、筆電等）註冊為「地球公物」(Earth Goods)，永久記錄於區塊鏈。

[Execution_Details]
- Platform: Mobile-first web platform syncing FB posts.
- Search System: Tagging and searching available items (Art/Tech focus).
- Blockchain: NFT-based identity on Zora/Polygon.
- Story Tracking: Record donor, donation date, borrowing history, and associated artistic projects.
- Borrowing: Users submit short motivation statements to borrow.

[Budget_Plan] Total 60,000 NTD
- Web/UI Design: 25,000
- Blockchain Minting (NFT): 10,000
- FB Content Integration: 7,000
- Documentation/Video: 6,000
- Hosting/Domain: 4,000
- Project Management: 8,000

[Expected_Outputs]
- Open infrastructure for shared resources.
- Permanent record of object life cycles.
- Transparent circulation of creative tools.
