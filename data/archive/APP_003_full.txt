✅ 提案名稱
大量交織+地球公物:透過區塊鏈設計橋接共享資源並實現公共化

✅ 計畫摘要
本計畫旨在將「大量交織」這個原本運作於 Facebook 社群的資源共享行動(目前社團已有超過
9,800 名成員),轉化為一個可搜尋、具資料庫架構的網頁平台。社團連結:
https://www.facebook.com/share/g/14DuoMUvodr/
此平台將能橋接全球的免費資源,並將其中部分資源註冊為「地球公物」(Earth Goods),成為永
久記錄於區塊鏈上的公共財產。
使用者可以搜尋共享物件(例如筆電、相機、繪圖板),查詢其是否可用(如:「使用中,預計 2025
年 5 月 23 日釋出」),並追蹤其過往使用紀錄。每件物品將記錄其捐贈者姓名、捐贈時間、借用紀
錄,以及其參與過的藝術或社區活動。
計畫目標是打造一個去中心化、具包容性的共享資源基礎設施,確保無論出生地或家庭背景,人
人皆可獲得基本的支持。

✅ 執行計畫
本專案將以開放協作方式推動,而非由單一技術團隊主導。提案人並不具備程式開發專業,因此
此提案同時也是一項邀請:希望 FAB DAO 中具備程式與設計能力的成員共同參與開發,透過共
創實現「大量交織+地球公物」平台。
● 開發手機優先介面的網站,將部分 Facebook 社團貼文同步至可搜尋的資料系統中。
● 使用者可上傳、分類標籤、搜尋可用物件(以藝術相關資源為起點,如相機、筆電、繪圖
板)。
● 部分具故事性的物件將註冊為區塊鏈 NFT(以 Zora 和 Polygon 為基礎),成為「地球公
物」。
● 建置可即時查詢的物件可及性模組(如是否可取用、預約日期)。
● 每件物建立專屬故事頁面,包含:
○ 捐贈者姓名
○ 捐贈日期
○ 借用紀錄(含使用者與地點)
○ 參與的公共或藝術專案紀錄
● 開放借用申請:使用者可簡述使用動機提出借用請求。

✅ Proposal Title
MEGAWEAVING + Earth Goods: Bridging Resources through Blockchain Commons

✅ Project Summary
This project seeks to transform the "MEGAWEAVING" resource-sharing ecosystem — originally
launched from a Facebook community that currently has over 9,800 members — see the
Facebook group here: https://www.facebook.com/share/g/14DuoMUvodr/ — into a searchable,
database-driven web platform. It will bridge global free resources and publicly register certain
items as Earth Goods — permanent, blockchain-based commons.
The platform allows users to search for community-shared items (such as laptops, cameras, or
drawing tablets), check availability (e.g., “in use until May 23, 2025”), and trace their usage
history. Each object is recorded with its donor’s name, donation date, usage history, and any
contribution to artworks or public activities.
The aim is to create a decentralized, inclusive infrastructure of shared resources to ensure that
no person is excluded from essential support simply due to birthplace or family background.

✅ Execution Plan
This project is envisioned as a participatory collaboration, not just a technical implementation.
As the proposer does not specialize in programming or web development, the intent of
submitting this grant is to invite FAB DAO members — developers, designers, and Web3
builders — to co-create this system together. Through open collaboration, we aim to collectively
shape and realize the MEGAWEAVING + Earth Goods platform.
● Develop a mobile-first web platform to sync selected Facebook posts into a tagged,
filterable database.
● Allow users to upload, tag, and search for available resources within art-related
categories (e.g. camera, laptop, tablet, etc.).
● Design a blockchain identity system (NFT-based on Zora and Polygon) for selected
items to become "Earth Goods."
● Implement a real-time availability module to indicate whether an item is usable or
reserved.
● Create dedicated item story pages that include:
○ donor name
○ donation date
○ timeline of borrowings (user + location)
○ public/artistic project associations
● Allow users to request to borrow, with a short statement explaining use.

✅ Expected Impact
● Provide a practical, beautifully designed entry point for Web3-based resource sharing.
● Record donor names, histories, and contributions permanently, emphasizing
participatory storytelling.
● Enable transparent circulation of creative tools and everyday equipment via blockchain
identity.
● Allow any child born anywhere to benefit from a growing commons of global shared
tools.
● Reinforce the role of open platforms in building material infrastructures of care and
cultural support.
● Earth Goods itself will evolve into a compelling participatory art project, where each
object is elevated by its history and symbolic value. As these objects are passed from
hand to hand, participating in different projects and lives, their stories will be
continuously recorded. Over time, these storylines will interweave, becoming a collective
tapestry of human connection and inspiration.
