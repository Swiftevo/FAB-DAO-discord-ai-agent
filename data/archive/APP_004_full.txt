[Core_Concept]
以「水環境」為主題的 IG 自媒體計畫。透過內容創作提升大眾對環境議題的基本素養，將台灣水環境（河流、污水處理、再生水）置於國際脈絡中觀察，反思數位治理的可能性。

[Execution_Details]
- Content Format: 150-300 words + 3-4 slides + 20s video per post.
- Topics: Science (turbidity, science facts), Global (Singapore, Spain water issues), History (Kaohsiung Love River), and Memory.
- Locations for Research: Taitung (groundwater), Taichung (leakage rates), Matsu (desalination), Yilan (no reservoirs).
- Goals: 10 posts in 6 months, aiming for 50 shares per post.

[Budget_Plan] Total 25,000 NTD
- Preparation/Positioning: 3,000
- Mid-term Execution (Travel, Interviews, Operations): 19,000
- Final Analysis/Fan Interaction: 3,000

[Expected_Outputs]
- 10 high-quality environmental education posts.
- Exploration of digital governance (e.g., Tamsui River transparency platform).
